What is Garbage Jiujitsu?

Here at Garbage Jiujitsu we believe that everyone should not only learn how to defend themselves, but also have fun doing it.
Their is no pressure here to become a world champion. Our goal for students here is to imrprove every class, and have a ton of fun doing it.
Whether you want to compete, learn jiujitsu to increase your confidence, or you just want to get in shape, Garbage Jiujitsu
is the place for you! (P.S. No big egos here, it's just jiujitsu. Don't take it so seriously).
